Game Info

In this free math game for kids, players have just 10 guesses to discover a secret number. Players type their guess into the phone and are told whether the secret number is "more than" or "less than" the number they guessed. There are three levels of difficulty: two levels with just positive numbers, and one level with positive and negative numbers. Use this game to practice basic math skills such as "more than," "less than," and positive and negative numbers!

About

Guess the number games are a fun and simple way to challenge your mind and have a good time.

Objective: The player has to guess a secret number within a certain range.

    Rules:
  • The game selects a random number within a defined range.
  • The player makes guesses trying to figure out the secret number.
  • For each guess, the game gives feedback whether the guess is too high, too low, or correct.
  • The game continues until the player guesses the number correctly.
